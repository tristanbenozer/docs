Every Hydra workflow — Discovery, Audit, Fix, Task, Improve, PR Review, and Govern — runs on AWS Bedrock, managed by Hydra. You do not need an Anthropic account, an API key, or any provider setup to use Hydra.

## What this means in practice

**Nothing to configure.** Compute is included in your Hydra plan. Connect your repo, run a workflow, and the AI runs.

**No surprise bills from a third party.** All compute cost is either included in your plan (Free) or billed as credits through Hydra (Pro, Team). One invoice.

**SOC 2 compliant infrastructure.** All inference runs on AWS Bedrock in a SOC 2-compliant environment. Your code is processed securely and is never used to train models.

## Models

| Plan | Models available |

Pro and Team plans include compute credits. Credits cover AI inference costs across all workflows you run. Top up from your Hydra dashboard at any time.

## Compute credits

Pro ($30/mo) and Team ($60/seat/mo) plans include a compute credit allowance matching the plan price. Credits are consumed as you run workflows. You can see your credit balance and per-workflow consumption in the dashboard.

Unused credits do not roll over. If you need more, top up from the billing settings.

## Data handling

Your code is sent to AWS Bedrock for inference and is not retained after the workflow completes. It is not used to train models. For compliance documentation, contact us.
