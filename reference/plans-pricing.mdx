- Full Discovery and Audit

- CLAUDE.md injection
- Public and private repos
- Model: Qwen 2.5 (Hydra-managed)

After 5 fixes, Discovery and Audit are also paused until the next billing cycle. Upgrade to Pro to remove the limit.

Everything in Free, plus:

- Up to 3 repositories
- Full-depth audit — all findings, all severity levels

- $30 in compute credits included monthly

Everything in Pro, plus:

- Team analytics + role-based access

- SSO / SAML
- Model: Claude Sonnet 4.6 via AWS Bedrock
- $60 in compute credits per seat included monthly

## Common questions

**Do I need a credit card for the free plan?**

No. The free plan requires no payment method.

**What happens when I hit 5 fixes on the free plan?**

Fix, Audit, and Discovery are paused until the next billing cycle. No overage charges.

**Can I have Viewers who don't count as paid seats?**

Yes. Viewer seats are free on all plans. Only Editor and Admin seats are counted toward the Team seat price.

**What is the annual discount?**

Annual billing on Pro and Team is 20% off the monthly price ($24/mo for Pro, $48/seat/mo for Team).

**How do I cancel?**

From **Settings > Billing**. Your plan stays active through the end of the current billing period.
